Non-negotiables for the 10 kg transformation
  • 01
    Protein first, every meal. 170–180g protein per day is non-negotiable. It's what keeps your 59.5 kg of muscle intact while fat drops. Every meal should start with the protein source.
  • 02
    Cardio finishers are mandatory. Every gym session ends with 15–20 min cardio. This is where the extra 200–300 kcal/day burn comes from. Don't skip it because you're tired from lifting.
  • 03
    Buy whey protein — it's not a luxury. You can't hit 175g from food alone with limited cooking time. MuscleBlaze Biozyme, 1 kg bag. Non-negotiable purchase for this plan.
  • 04
    Visceral fat (score 11) is your health priority. It drops fastest with calorie deficit + compound lifting + cardio. You're already set up for all three — just be consistent.
  • 05
    Weigh yourself every Sunday morning, fasted. Use 7-day rolling average, not single readings. Weight fluctuates 1–2 kg daily from water and food. Don't panic at single readings.
  • 06
    Sleep 7–8 hours. Under 6 hrs = cortisol spikes, hunger hormones spike, fat burning slows. Sleep is a training variable, not a bonus.
  • 07
    Expect fast drop weeks 1–2, then slower. First 2 weeks may drop 2–3 kg — mostly water and glycogen. Real fat loss shows from week 3 at 0.8–1 kg/week. Don't be discouraged by the plateau after the initial drop.
  • 08
    Home plan when hometown, gym plan when in Kochi. Don't skip because you can't go to the gym. The home plan is designed to maintain progress. Consistency > perfection.
  • 09
    Set a 50-min standing alarm at work. Walk 5 min every hour. Adds 300–400 kcal of NEAT to your daily burn. The easiest calorie burn available to you.
  • 10
    When hungry, add protein not carbs. You will feel hungry in a deficit. Add an egg or 50g of chicken — not more rice. This keeps you full longer and protects muscle.
